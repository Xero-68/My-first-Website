    <h1>Traveling with Hearing Loss</h1>
    <p>Traveling with hearing loss can be easier with a bit of preparation. With the right tools and planning, your journey can be smoother and more enjoyable.</p>

    <div class="tip">
        <h2>Helpful Tips:</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Use alert systems:</strong> These can help you stay aware of important announcements at airports or stations.</li>
            <li><strong>Download mobile apps:</strong> Many travel and communication apps offer accessibility features like live captions or text-based alerts.</li>
            <li><strong>Plan school or group trips with support:</strong> Ensure there are accommodations for hearing needs in group travel settings.</li>
            <li><strong>Request accessible accommodations:</strong> Hotels and transport providers may offer visual alerts or assistive devices if asked in advance.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <p>With these strategies, traveling becomes less stressful and much more enjoyable for individuals with hearing loss.</p>
